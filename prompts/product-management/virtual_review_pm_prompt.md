## Gate Decision Vocabulary

Use one of the following decisions when reviewing product stage outputs:
- accepted
- accepted-with-constraints
- needs-more-research
- blocked-by-architecture
- rejected

If research evidence is insufficient, use `needs-more-research`.
If service boundaries or shared capability ownership are not confirmed, use `blocked-by-architecture`.
Every non-accepted result must include rework targets, missing evidence, and the stage to return to.
